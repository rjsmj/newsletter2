A Message from Robert Carrillo

FOUNDER & PRESIDENT | THE LAUNCHPAD COLLECTIVE, INC.

Dear Friends,

I want to personally thank each one of our investors, partners, and supporters for your continued belief in LaunchPad's mission. Your trust and contributions make our shared vision a reality — transforming opportunity into impact for the San Bernardino community.

We know we can't do this alone. LaunchPad's success is built on the collective efforts of passionate individuals who believe in creating lasting change and ensuring our community not only survives, but thrives.

In this edition, we're excited to share highlights from the 2025 LaunchPad Gala, a replay of our Growth Fund webinar, and the launch of our new Fireside Chat Series featuring our incredible community partners.

We're also moving forward on the Del Rosa LaunchPad — our flagship site. Our site plan is nearing completion, modular coordination is underway, and we are preparing for entitlement in early 2026. This project will bring dignified housing, small-business opportunities, and living-wage jobs to the heart of San Bernardino.

And on a personal note, I'm proud to share that LUCHA: A Wrestling Tale is now streaming on Apple TV and Amazon.

We're building more than buildings — we're building momentum. Thank you for being part of it.

Warm regards,

Robert Carrillo
